小雪

小雪是二十四节气之一，通常出现在每年的 11 月 22 日或 23 日。小雪标志着冬季的进一步深入，气温逐渐下降，开始出现轻微的降雪。小雪的气候特点是寒冷、干燥，自然界逐渐进入休眠状态。在中国传统文化中，小雪象征着冬季的正式开始，人们开始为寒冷的冬季做准备。

Xiaoxue is one of the 24 solar terms, usually occurring on November 22 or 23 each year. Xiaoxue marks the deepening of winter, with the temperature gradually dropping and light snowfall beginning to occur. The climate of Xiaoxue is characterized by cold and dry weather, with nature gradually entering a dormant state. In traditional Chinese culture, Xiaoxue symbolizes the official start of winter, and people begin to prepare for the cold winter.

春分

春分是二十四节气之一，通常出现在每年的 3 月 20 日或 21 日，是春季的中点。春分时，昼夜几乎等长，标志着春天的正式开始。春分的气候特点是温暖、湿润，自然界开始复苏，万物生长。在中国传统文化中，春分象征着平衡和和谐，是播种和农耕的重要时节。

The Spring Equinox is one of the 24 solar terms, usually occurring on March 20 or 21 each year, marking the midpoint of spring. During the Spring Equinox, the length of day and night is almost equal, marking the official start of spring. The climate during the Spring Equinox is characterized by warm and humid weather, with nature beginning to revive and everything growing. In traditional Chinese culture, the Spring Equinox symbolizes balance and harmony, and is an important time for sowing and farming.
